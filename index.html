Design For The Public 2024

A two-day event exploring public interest design, hosted at Revolution Hall and the Wacom Experience Center in Portland, OR. Organized by Ron Bronson and Technologists for the Public Good.

🎤 Agenda Highlights

  • Keynote: Ron Bronson — "Designing the Moment: The Current State and Future of Public Interest Design"
  • Feature Talk: Christian Crumlish — "Bridging Design and Product Management in Government"
  • Panel: Erin Stevanus, Tiffany Sun, et al. — "Navigating Career Paths for Designers"
  • Closing Talk: Dr. Shelly Ronen — "Fostering Public Service Design: Tackling Challenges in Child Welfare"
  • Collaboration Panel: Olivia Olson, Nicole Mors, Elise Nelson Leary — "Breaking Silos: Effective Collaboration with Stakeholders"
  • Show & Tell and Design Crit sessions facilitated across both days
  • Workshops including "Trauma-Informed Design for Public Services"
